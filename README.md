# HR-Analytics-Dashboard
Project Description: HR Analytics Dashboard
Goal: Develop an HR Analytics Dashboard focused on key performance indicators (KPIs) and a comprehensive analysis of employee attrition to drive data-informed retention strategies.
Key Insights & Metrics:
Total Employees: 1,470
Attrition Rate: $\approx 16.12\%$ (237 leavers)
Average Age: $\approx 36.9$ years
Core Analysis:The dashboard segments attrition across critical dimensions to identify risk areas:
Demographics: Attrition rates broken down by Age Group (highest in the 25-34 band), Gender (60% Male / 40% Female workforce split), and Marital Status.
Organizational: Analysis of attrition concentration by Department (R&D and Sales are major contributors) and specific Job Roles (e.g., Laboratory Technician, Sales Executive).
Employee Profile: Attrition based on Education Level and tracking of average Job Satisfaction.
